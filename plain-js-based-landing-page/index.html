The Materials

Raw textures,
refined purpose.

Cork

Alentejo, Portugal

Harvested from the bark of Quercus suber without harming the tree. Each oak is stripped only once every nine years. Naturally waterproof, antimicrobial, and completely renewable.

Silk

Assam, India

Peace silk (Ahimsa) — harvested after the moth has emerged naturally. Stronger than steel by weight. Thermoregulating. The oldest luxury textile known to civilization.

Wool

Arles, France

Merino wool from free-range flocks in Provence. Naturally flame-resistant, moisture-wicking, and odor-resistant. Biodegrades fully within 12 months.
